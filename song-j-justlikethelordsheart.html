His grace that brought light to my eyes
So thankful for the love He gave me
Although He’s loved~ me,
My life has still remained unclean
He~ sees me and cries out with tears on my~ life

When I look in His heart
He’s perfect in every way I see
So radiant, brilliant to my eyes
And so I ask You Lord,
Will You please use that light of Yours

With the fire of love
Burn away all my flaws and sins
With water of grace
Lord~ please make my spirit clean
I am filled with all these~ sins~

With the fire of love
Burn away all my flaws and sins

Lord~ please make my spirit clean
I~ want a heart like~ yours

O Lord

O Lord please come to me

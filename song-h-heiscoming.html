He is Coming

Verse 1
The day when the Lord's to come is drawing nearer day to day to day
Dressed in radiant clothes, the ready souls wait for the Lord to come
Purpose of creation, my spirit is changed, goin' to Heaven
Born again as a bride, I will love forever our Creator

Verse 2 I will push myself and I will live within the Word of the Lord
I'll bear the fruit of love, do it by repenting passionately
Purpose of creation, my spirit complete, goin' to Heaven
I will praise He who made me His bride, I'll praise our Creator

*The Lord comes back again, that day might be tomorrow
It comes once in a thousand years, I'll receive Him on that day

**The trumpet call of the Lord's Word, I'll listen 'n' act on it
With changed deeds, I'll be revived, my spirit
The archangels' trumpet call, I'll hear it rapture high
My spirit changed, it's a wedding banquet with the Lord

Rap part (overlap)
I prepare for the day when the Lord's to come
The Lord is to come again, my spirit will follow Him
Happens once in a thousand years
All of us will receive the Lord on that day

He's Come!
He's come!

Pray

The prayers you prayed for yourself, have been fulfilled;
If you pray for your brothers and your nation, they will also come true.

Never has there been a time on earth when prayer was needed as now
So pray every day, night and day, I will answer them all.

You must make me the first priority above all in your life
Know that I bought~ you with my life; you belong to me

Even the extended time is now drawing very fast to an end
Repent urgently of all your sins~, and prepare yourself.

The Lord’s return will happen much sooner, much sooner than you think
Really, spare your time; use it wisely. And make yourself clean

I am your God of heaven and earth, Yahweh; I am Jesus Christ
The Savior, Jehovah, King of Kings, and Lord of Lords.

(CHORUS)
Therefore, pray, standing on faith firm and strong!
With deep sorrow, pray! With~ true sincerity, pray!

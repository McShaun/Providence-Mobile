Blessing

My love who is rising like the sun;
my love who is lovely as the moon

God's purpose of creation,
My love who has been matched for me by the Lord

Oh blossom with the Will of the Heavens;

forever into the flower of love
Love of the Lord, let it be ablaze

In my heart, O my love, Oh love of my Lord.

Aha ~ love should be as hot as the sun

Aha ~ love should be as gentle as the moon

Aha ~ love that's been joined by the Heavens

Oh, I shall never ever forget you
Oh my love, oh my Lord's love

Forever, can't forget, can't forget

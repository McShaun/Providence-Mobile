Life

When I become a grand tree and blossom the leaves and the flowers
I shall make the bees and butterflies crowd ‘n’ fly my way
When I become autumn and bear fruit in clusters ‘n’ clusters
With baskets on their heads and wicker on their backs
I shall gather everyone just like the clouds

You go ahead dig deeper
And don’t spare any fertile soil and water at all
Your sweat drops of hard effort like showers of rain
Let’em run down from your head to the ends of your feet

At that time, we shall fulfill our joyful land
A land without hatred nor war
At that time, we shall fulfill our land to be
A land of happiness

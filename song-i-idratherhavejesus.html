I'd Rather Have Jesus

There’s nothing more precious than my dear Lord
I cannot trade Him for the wealth of the world
I cannot forget the amazing love
Of the Lord who saved my body and spirit

There’s nothing more precious than my dear Lord
I cannot trade Him for world-wide fame
Even the pleasures of past worldly lives
Cannot take from me this love for Him

There’s nothing more precious than my dear Lord
I can’t trade Him for the joys of the world
Even persecutions and temptations
Can never change my heart that serves the Lord

I’ve cast away all the worldly joys
I’ve cast away all its pride
There’s nothing more precious than my dear Lord
There is only the Lord

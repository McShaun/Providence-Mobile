To Fly

1. Let’s cast our net of lives to the sea
Let’s save the lost brides of the Lord
The Lord inspires us, I’ll do as He leads me
Together with the Lord fulfill the Will

Let’s cast the net of the Word of God as one
Throw the nets of the gospel as one
Let’s spread the true power of the Word
To the future
To the heaven’s gates
With our hopes, let’s soar to the sky

Spreading our wings
Seize His hand, to the path of life
And fly
Soar above the sky
Higher
With the Lord’s will in our hearts

Fly high up in the sky

Spreading our wings
Seize His hand, to the path of life
And fly
Soar above the sky
Farther
With the Lord’s love in our hearts

Tell the world, shout it out
With the Lord

2. Live day by day, above and beyond
Overcome ourselves and let us fly
Feeling the Lord’s heart, with urgency we go
Together with the Lord fulfill the will

Embrace the Spirit and with all our hearts
Let’s fly with a self that is changed
Let’s shout out to the world
That He will come again

Boldly with love, let us tell the world

Reflection

The Lord I’ve waited for a thousand years as if a day 
Because I cannot sit still like a fool
Looking up at the clouds floating by in the sky
To my heart’s content I sing of the Lord who has come
Thinking about the Lord I could not fall asleep; meditating, 
I think of the Word.
The Words relentlessly cried to groups small and big
Those Words of life I forever, I forever love

My dear beloved one, please lend your ears
Hist’ry of the Heavens is being fulfilled on the earth
My dear beloved one, please open your eyes
The Lord whom we have been waiting for, stands in front of us.

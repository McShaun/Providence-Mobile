Whatever Love May Be

Whatever love may be, whatever truth may be, though we cannot understand yet
When time has flowed by, then everyone will jump for joy, we do not know it now
There is eternally no regret in truth and love, you who do not know the Will
If you live on your own without loving the Lord, you will regret even more

I shall love the Lord, though difficult; I shall love the Lord eternally
Providence I’ve grown to love, though difficult; I shall love only Providence

Whatever truth may be, whatever love may be, we are able to know it now
Those who are even today, just spectators of the Lord, they will never understand
Those who have lived their lives all say it in this way, whatever truth may be
Whatever love may be, it’s an eternal secret, one can’t know on his own 

I shall love the Lord, though difficult; I shall love the Lord eternally
Providence I’ve grown to love, though difficult; I shall love only Providence
Why regret on the way of Providence
Life in the Lord’s embrace, though difficult, still I shall love only you.

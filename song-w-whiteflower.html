White Flower

1.Alone, I come quietly
Unto the Lord, I want to confess everything
All that I felt too ashamed to tell anyone
Words that are filled with tears

As I live in~ this world
All the burden of my sins~ weigh down on me
This~ heavy burden keeps dragging me down
So I hold the Lord’s hand

* Oh, my dear Lord, oh, Lord Jesus
Please accept this earnest confession that I make
I will never, never ever
Make~ Your heart be filled with pain again
Oh my dear Lord, oh, Lord Jesus
There’s no one else besides You who can make me clean
Please accept me, please accept me
Will You please accept me although I fall short

2.Oh Lord, with love You’ve raised me
You have raised me up with hopes that I would be~ great
Now I want to bring You joy and no more sorrows
Please take hold of my hand (*)

Oh, my dear Lord, oh, Lord Jesus
Please accept this earnest confession that I make
There’s no other love like Yours
There is no other love that makes miracles
Jesus You have forgiven me
of all the sins my tears alone won’t wash away
Ever thankful, I love You, Lord
I will become a white flower for You, Lord
I will become a white flower for You, Lord

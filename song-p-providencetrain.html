Providence Train

Oh, I wanted to show my~self beautifully to the Lord 
Among those who were waving their hands
I waved mine as well 
The more I realize
He is the Lord, the more tears I seem to shed
If he is my love though time may pass, truly he will not forget me.  
The love of Providence gives me joy and hope 
Forever in Providence I’ll live my life loving the Lord
If one day I receive my lover’s phone call (letter) 
I will not be able to fall asleep 
With only the thought of my love time passes by 
My lover loving me as I love him
 If it is the Lord will he forget me in the flow of time 
If I live my life waiting for him, there will come a day when we'll meet

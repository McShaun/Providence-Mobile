My Joy and My Hope

The Lord~ who is the~ source of my joy and hope
The Lord who has become my life
I may sing praises to Him all day and night
Oh, it never seems to be enough

My good Shepherd whom~ I so dearly adore
Somewhere in a fragrant garden
Will He always walk with His sheep and His flock
And~ share His joy along with them all

To all the brides who live in the world of Providence
My Lord so tender, full of love
Have you ever met Him in a wilderness
Or ever met Him in the Holy Land

On the~ harsh and broad wasteland without a path
An image who can’t find the way
‘Tis they who are lost wandering to and fro
And the enemy is mocking them

The merciful and pea~ceful face of~ the Lord
A face all the angels welcome
And the whole world delights in the Lord’s words
Those astonishing words of truth

My precious Lord whom I so truly love
I delight even at Your face
The only source of my life and all my hope
Is only my Lord and no one else

Is only my Lord and no one else

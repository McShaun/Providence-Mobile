Soldier's Song of Providence

There are so many things to do, born as a human being
You and I lived with the glory of guarding Providence
Brotherhood bonded in the heart and with the word
As the sun rises and the sun sets on the mountain tops
Heaven and earth, trusting you, fulfill God’s history

There are so many things to do being back in Providence
You and I lived steadfast preaching the new word
Brotherhood bonded with love and with the truth
As we run hard to fulfill the Will in Providence
Heaven and Earth, even now, fulfill God’s history

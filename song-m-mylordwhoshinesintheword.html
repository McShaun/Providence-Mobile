My Joy and My Hope

Verse 1
My Lord who shines in the Word, He speaks with the Holy Spirit
With justice My Lord rules, Oh, He is only love~
My Lord has come, I'll celebrate. He came to judge with words of truth
He came here, the earth in the sky. Will they ever know?

Verse 2
My Lord came with the Word of Truth, We praise Him with joy and gladness
Shine your light of wisdom and knowledge, listen to the Word
He has come right by our side, He came to save all of us
Listen to the words of wisdom, realize, act on them

Verse 3
My Lord came with the Word, He speaks with lips of fire
He condemns all Satans, Oh, who could He be?
My Lord came here on earth, He came to save this world
If we meet the Lord in ignorance, we'll be at a loss

Verse 4
My Lord came in the midst of darkness and He brought light upon us all
He brought to life our spirits once dead, Oh, who could He be?
He revived those who were dead and saved His people
He truly brought me to life, I shall give Him my all

Verse 5
My Lord called us all to fulfill the purpose of creation
My Lord fulfills Heaven on Earth, there's only joy
My Lord judges justly, He speaks words of truth and love
My Lord gives us as much as we do, I shall follow Him

Verse 6
My Lord came like lightening, He proclaims the new truth
My Lord raptures us into the sky, I shall follow Him
He came to us from the East, flowers bloom in Korea (our Land)
Let's build up a land of love, a land of truth

Chorus
Until I met the Lord face to face, it was only sorrow and pain
My Lord came for me, for my sake, I know that now, He was with me all the while
My Lord is by my side, My Lord who keeps me safe
With the Lord who is my life, I~ shall live forever
I shall live forever

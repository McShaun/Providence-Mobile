Without the Lord, I Cannot Live On

I can’t understand the reason for their laughter
They say that they do not need the Lord to be happy
Without the Lord, I cannot breathe at all
Without the Lord, I cannot stand to be

The true happiness that the world desires
Has nothing to do with the Lord or eternal love
But I only want the Lord’s love
To be the object of the Lord’s love

The Lord is within me and I am within the Lord
I’ve got the world as long as I have the Lord
With the Lord with me, I’m so happy
I’m so thankful that He loves me

With the Lord with me, I can do anything
With the Lord with me, there is nothing I shall fear
Oh, my love, please take notice of me
Oh, my Love, please don’t leave me behind

(Chorus)
Without Him, I can’t live on, I cannot live all alone
All that I breathe is for Him, He’s my everything
The whole wide world may change but this heart will never change
Without You, Lord, I cannot live on

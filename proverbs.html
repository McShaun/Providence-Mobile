Proverbs about Heaven

"Heaven is made according to the desires of the Trinity. They want those who love them the most to come and live there.
Even you want to give the best things to those who live devote their body and love you the most among those who love you. You give to them and let them use it.
Things of the world are things given on a miniature scale as preparation to give eternal things in Heaven."
"Your location in Heaven is determined by your thoughts, actions, and accomplishments. Even in the same location, your position is determined by how much merit you accumulated. However, even there, if you like water, he will let you live with background of water, and if you like mountains, he will let you live with background of mountains. You definitely live in a world that is a manifestation of your thoughts, actions, ways, and accomplishments."

- JMS, May 22nd 2012

Proverbs about "flying"

It's not that a big thing cannot fly because it's heavy or a small thing can fly because it's light. Regardless of size, it flies because it was created to fly.
Though big and heavy, earth flies in the Universe, but a stone, though small, cannot fly even on earth. Stars too fly in the Universe.
The heart should fly in the midst of life.
'To fly' means 'to free from restriction.'
Just as creatures with wings live flying, human beings also should live flying in their respective field.
There are people who fly by studying and making efforts on their own and there are people who fly by living with the Almighty who created them and with the savior who saves them.
Human beings should fly in the body, fly with the mind, fly in the spirit, and fly leading a life that enables their spirit to live forever. Only they will reach the greatest heights.
If you live without being in love with the Lord who came to save the world, you cannot live your life flying.
Even within the same region, some live flying, some live walking, and some live crawling. It is not known until that life is lived.
According to the deeds of your body, the life of your body on earth, as well the life of your spirit in the spiritual world, is formed.

- JMS, April 2012

Proverbs about healing

One person thought that a ghost was making him sick. All he did was pray for the ghosts to be gone. When he was examined medically, [it turned out that] he was hurting because germs had invaded a wound. He was immediately healed when the wound was disinfected and medication was applied.
There was a person who thought that they were in that much pain because one side of his body was rotting. He went to the hospital and got x-rayed only to find that there was nothing wrong. Demons and ghosts do not show up on x-rays. Ultimately they came out through prayer.

- JMS, Thursday May 17, 2012

Proverbs about mind and body

A person's mind is "life" for the body. If you're going to communicate with the Lord, your mentality must be connected to Him. To be "connected" means you must hook your heart to the Lord's heart. Once you're linked, you will get through to Him through conversation.
A person's body cannot take anything with them when they die. Likewise, you can't take anything with you when you go to Heaven. Your spirits can take to Heaven only the righteous deeds and accomplishments you've done in the world. Such things are love, truth, and goodness. Once your spirit is transformed into a person of Heaven through love, truth, and goodness, you take your changed spirit with you to Heaven along with knowledge and wisdom about Heaven you've gained while the body was in the world.
When constructing a large building the tools used by the workers are different, the types of workers are different, and the work each worker does is different. All of them must work together as one. Then the building will become complete. In the same way, those who are working for the Lord must work together as one and be understanding of each other even though their characters, personalities, and work are different. If they do, goals are achieved and work gets done. If everyone does the exact same work, the building will not be completed even in an entire lifetime. Everyone must help, care for, and love as they build the building while thinking, "This is a house I will live in."
Your mind and actions have to be totally consumed with love for the Lord. Then you will not love anything in the world and you will not fall for those things.

- JMS, Monday March 14th

In life there are many times people say things that upset you and create conflicts with you. Don't store memories of such things in your brain, don't be aware of them, only plug your ears, ignore them, and move on!

Proverbs about the body, soul, and spirit

When you are calling out to, praying, and petitioning to the Almighty Holy Son of the Trinity, and focus on the Holy Son the Lord, you will gain realizations about His heart.
The body cannot connect with the spirit by itself. Even your own body has to go through the heart, mind, thoughts and soul in order to connect to the spirit. And even if it is your own spirit, it must do the physical work through the body.
When you see your spirit in a dream, what you're seeing is a supernatural effect in which your heart, mind, and soul appear and take action with the body's image as the basis. That is not your spirit's actual image. That's an image of the spirit you see through the heart, mind, and soul of your body.

- JMS, Thursday May 10th 2012

Proverbs about 'running until the end'

A successful person keep running only on the path of success without giving up on it, even if they have to give up on all other things so that they can continue to run on this path.
Whatever area it may be, when a person becomes an expert, they try it scores of times (the first stage), they try it hundreds of times next (the second stage), they try it thousands of times (the third stage), and then they try tens of thousands of times (the fourth stage). After these many attempts, when they are able to do it without a mistake, they become an expert.
Until 6, you have to wrestle with Satan. The side of Heaven is number 7. Therefore, as long as they go to the end, they will win. The level which is one step higher than number 6 is the number of love. If you love the Holy Son Lord 100%, you reach number 7. The number of the one thousand year Complete Testament history, 7, which is the last number, is the number of a thousand year wedding banquet of love. It is the number of love. Therefore, whoever hopes to defeat Satan must raise themselves one level higher than Satan's number and love only the Lord with number 7, that is the number of love.
Up to number 6, it is about faith, Word, praise, thanksgiving, prayer, endurance, and obedience. Despite all that, Satan wrestles with us. If you add the last 1 to number 6, then it becomes number 7. And that [one number] is 'love.' Satan cannot love, so he ends at number 6. When you just love the Holy Son Lord, the things of Satan end there. Satan is opposite to love, so if we just love the Lord, it means the end for Satan.
It is about earnest prayer and love.
Do not just have an earnest heart but take action with an earnest heart. Things work as you make up your mind and take action.
The sound of truthfulness and sincerity sets one's heart on fire. Therefore, it does not stop there but actualizes what is in the heart in reality.
Having an earnest heart is like making something with complete devotion.
The Holy Son Lord is sincere and truthful. Therefore, when human beings are sincere and truthful, they connect with the Lord.
There is the ultimate difference between Satan and us--that is, Satan does not love the Holy Son Lord. Therefore, it is easy for Satan to tempt us if we only try to be saved by the Lord while not truly loving the Lord.

- JMS, Saturday, May 5, 2012

Proverbs about fulfilling hope

Whether you live in a palace, a grass-thatched house, confined in a prison, or are going through any suffering or tribulation; you should not lose hope in the present and live, doing every day what you must do and fulfilling the Lord's will in your current environment and position. If you fail to receive what you should receive just because you are going through sufferings in the present, you will not be able to receive it even if a peaceful time comes in the future.
The hope of the future is fulfilled in the present.
What was hoped for in the past is also fulfilled in the present.

- JMS, Tuesday March 22, 2011

Past, Present, Future

A life of hope that everyone dreamed would take place in the future is being lived everyday in the reality now.
Unless we fulfill the hope, dreams, and actions of the future in the reality now, they will not be fulfilled even in the future.
If you cannot understand, view yesterday as the past, view today as the present, and view tomorrow as the future. Unless you fulfill your hope for today, you will not be able to fulfill the hope of tomorrow. In a human being's life, the past, the present, and the future are all connected.

- JMS, Monday, March 21, 2011

Proverbs about the spirit

The fate of one's spirit is determined according to the person's heart, thoughts, and the action of their body. Only by listening to Jesus' perfect Word, deliberately making a determination, and taking action, will the body transform into a person of a heavenly level while the spirit, too, changes to the same level of heaven.

- Saturday, February 26, 2011

The spirit is made and perfected according to the body's deeds, thus it comes to receive love from the Lord. Therefore, if the body listens to the Lord's words and does righteous deeds, both the body and the spirit will change, shine brightly, and carry out a mission at a higher level.

- Friday, February 25, 2011

You don't gain things just because you try to gain more. You will make gains as much as your heart, thoughts, and actions change for the better according to the Lord's will.
When you have the same actions, same heart, and same things repeating over and over again, you will only receive things of that level and stage. You must change in better ways. Then you will receive better and different things.
How long will you not change and continue to live in the mud brick home? Do you intend to live only in your spiritual domain, according to your own thoughts and not the Lord's thoughts?
Some people are living in mansions of faith by hearing the Lord's words and taking action with all their might. Yet they are not satisfied and are struggling even more so they will change more. You follow the Lord with as much conviction as they do, but how long do you intend to live satisfied with the mud brick home?

- JMS, February 23, 2011

Proverbs about God's history

Understand that the 30 principles were words I received after virtually trading my own life as I struggled mightily for those arduous 30 years. Correctly knowing and understanding the words of God is not easy. I realized the words only after laying such foundations and conditions. I ran for the Lord, reading the bible 2000 times, evangelizing 10,000 people per year, defeating all the satans and demons, and overcoming all matters of physical adversities. So treacherous and wretched was my physical life that I cringe even at the thought of it.
Now that the word of this time period has been proclaimed, it is spreading. "Listen to the words of the Thirty Principles. The words of this time period have begun. God's history has begun."
Whether the person is suffering as a result of sins, laziness, or not receiving blessings from God, we must help and have mercy on them regardless of the reason. When Jesus came to this world with the mission from God to make salvation happen, he helped and comforted the poor, the destitute, the prostitutes, sinners, and everyone else in need. We must do the same in our lives so that we can enter the kingdom that God prepared and not be judged. Only by living that way can we shine brightly. If you love me and obey my words, then you will help those who love and follow me whenever they are in need, without pointing fingers and accusing them of failing to fulfill personal responsibilities, of being ignorant, lazy, or having weak faith. You must help them and watch over them as you would watch over your own bodies, and you must eat and rejoice with them.
Then you will know the truth and the truth will set you free. Jesus proclaimed the words of salvation from God for that new time period, and when anyone who was a prisoner of the former time period followed those words, then they became free. When the people came to know the truth, then they were freed from the bondage of the OT [Old Testament]. And, in the same way, those who were imprisoned by the spiritual domain of the NT [New Testament], by hearing the words of this time period and the 30 principles, are now free. They now have religious freedom, spiritual freedom, and physical freedom.
Make peace and resolve among you. Talk and maintain good relationships. If you cannot resolve conflicts with visible men, how could you resolve conflicts with invisible God? If you don't reconcile with one another, I myself do not resolve with you. In deep prayer, you realize all your problems can be attributed to your shortcomings. You have learned the 30 Lessons and the tremendous Word of Heaven, so you have become quite polished in terms of speaking. You have received all the necessary resources you need to be a man or woman of Heaven. As a mouth to preach the Gospel of the time, your mouth should not be tough. 'Tough' here means 'dirty,' or 'rotten'.

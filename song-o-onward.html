Onward

If it be~ that, Jesus gave me freedom to move
I would testify, till I can’t move no more
If it be~ that, there’s no one around to hear my words
With a pen I’ll shout, till I can’t write no more
If it be~ that, I’m confined and all alone
I’ll go b’fore the Lord, till I can’t kneel no more
If it be~ that, ev’ryone in the world blocked my way
I will still follow~~ / the path of the Lord

(Chorus)
Yes, onward I will go
No hard~ships will hold me down
I’ll do it for the Lord, I’ll do it for His Will
Yes, onward I will go
Though the whole~ world may block my way
I’ll give~ Him my all, and preach of His love
Yes, onward I will go

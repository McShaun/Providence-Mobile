Starting With Me

Just as the bright sun
Rises above
High in the sky
I won’t fall or sink
To the ground
Even in hardships
I’ll choose this path
The path of the Lord
I’ll walk in the light
Heaven-bound

I’ll look above
Towards the Lord
With unstoppable faith
I will shine
Upon this whole world
The dazzling love
Of the Lord

Holding on to His dream
I will now arise
With His righteous strength
I will now arise
Revival
And restoring this land
Is all
Here and now starting with me

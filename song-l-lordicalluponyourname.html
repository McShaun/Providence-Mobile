Lord I call Upon Your Name

Lord, I call upon your name, O my Jesus, O my Love
Lord, where are You, where are you now?
I want to be in your arms and confess to you, O Lord
At this moment I~ wish to be reborn

With all my heart I call and call, all of a sudden I can feel
The warmth of His love come closer to me
As the warmth of His love continuously seeps into my soul
Hot tears begin to stream down from my eyes

‘Cause I feel my Lord has come, I feel Him next to me
The glowing warmth of His love, is healing my spirit
How I want to be the love of my Lord
Earnestly I plead, “Forgive me,”
Je~sus, O, Je~sus, I call upon your name

O I feel my Lord has come, please stay right next to me
Open up your heart to me, O please receive me
For I’m weak and poor and miserable, Lord, my hope’s in you alone~
Je~sus, O, Je~sus
I call upon your name, O Je~sus
O my Jesus O my Love

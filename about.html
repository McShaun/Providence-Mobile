About

Providence Church

Our teacher began Providence Church 30 years ago to spread the gospel throughout the world. Providence has now spread to over 50 different nations. Our mission is to resurrect the arts, education based on the Word of God, and understanding of this world by encouraging a lifestyle of giving glory to God.

In this version of the app we have included

  • Gallery These are the symbols of Providence Church. We've included some original art, photography, and landscapes.
  • Featured Messages These sermons have been selected because they especially feature the key themes and doctrines unique to Providence church.
  • A Praisebook containing the songs inspired by and originally written within Providence church.

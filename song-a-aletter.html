A Letter Written Above the Clouds

I truly
Truly like the Lord
I just can’t help but smile
When I look at you
More than anyone I know
I love the Lord
I cherish Him more
than myself

Clasping my hands
With all my heart
I~ pray
For our love
To last oh, forever
To the Lord
I tell him that I love Him
This heart of mine
Flies to the sky
To reach your heart above

I just love you so much
My heart’s aflutter
And I write
A letter on the clouds
And the wind
Brings my heart up to you
I miss you so
And I cry
Then I write
A letter on the clouds
Just like rain
My tears drench your heart


The Lord is always
Right here by my side
Looking at me
He is smiling
brightly

I want to be
Embraced in His arms
Oh Lord I really love You
I want to be with You
Together forever
Whoa

The most precious one
In the whole world
Treasure of the heaven
That is You
Only You
My love, my Lord
Brightest of all
Deepest of all
Widest of all
Is Your heart, my Lord
My dear love
Jesus, my Lord
‘Til forever, I will love You
Lord

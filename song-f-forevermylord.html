Forever My Lord

The day I met the Lord I understood
Blossomed were the flowers of truth and love
My mind made up when we first met will be lasting forever, my Lord
Those lengthy two thousand years, those lengthy two thousand years
Were a winter of blizzards
Providence in happiness; flowers blooming in the storm
Joyful is my life in the New History!
As I gaze at the Lord, passing seasons like a day
Oh, where can I ever hear the voice of the Lord
This everlasting mind, this everlasting one
Shall never leave Providence

Just as a day comes when the night is gone
New Testament's gone and the Complete's here
The Lord who we've so waited for, Oh, the Lord of New History
Over mountain trails of thorns, over mountain trails of thorns
And across the oceans He has come *

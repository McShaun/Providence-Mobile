You Youthful One

Let us run to~ the field of Canaan;
Let us shine~ bright the sun of truth
To the land of Providence,
where all of us living here are brighter than gems;
we are fulfilling the hope of the millennium.

Oh, you youthful one wake up and come
Oh, you youthful one wake up and come
Ah~ ah~, oh you lovely youthful one
Ah~ ah~, oh you youthful one like the sun
You youthful one, you youthful one.

Come, running to the new world of New History
Let us fulfill the hopes of our youth
The Lord lives in Providence 
As he blows the trumpet of truth 
This beautiful world, it’s calling out to you 
To come to Providence

Always with the Lord

Everything I have done until this day
Were all the things that You have been doing through me
You used someone as lowly as me, oh, what grace
I thank You, Lord, eternally

Lord, You don’t have a body, I can feel Your pain
I want to be Your body forever without end
Teach me, please, till You cannot speak any more
I will act on Your words, till I can’t move any more

Be with me, Lord, for eternity
Be by my side, every mo~ment
Without You my Lord, I can’t do anything
I’ll do all things, following Your Will

Be with me, Lord, for eternity
Be by my side, every moment
To the ends~ of the earth, I’ll be with You
All I want is- to fulfill- Your every wish and Will

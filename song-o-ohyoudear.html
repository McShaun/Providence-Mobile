Oh You Dear

Do not be somber any more; Oh you dear dance and celebrate
In truth and liberty; Oh you dear, oh you dear, radiate your light
Now break forth and arise, rising up through the dark
And sing about the Lord of New Hist’ry
Shine bright, be radiant, even through darkest night
With the eternal truth, light up the world
On this earth heaven is now being fulfilled
Oh you dear, Oh you dear

Do not be worried any more; Oh you dear sing praises to Him
Embraced in love and peace; Oh you dear, oh you dear, radiate your light
Now break forth and fly, spreading your wings and rise
Stand up and sing new songs of Providence
Run forward, my dear love, in spite of wind and rain
With the eternal love, light up the world 
On this earth heaven is now being fulfilled
Oh you dear, Oh you dear
